01About

JK

I'm a master's student in Clinical Psychology at Seoul National University. I'm curious about how people regulate difficult emotions, and how that understanding can shape better, more accessible mental health care.

My current work looks at anxiety and mood-related difficulties, and how everyday tools — from self-report measures to digital interventions — can be designed to actually reflect a person's experience rather than flatten it.

Outside of research, I enjoy long walks, good coffee, and conversations that wander off-topic in useful ways.
